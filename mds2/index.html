It is recommended that this system be maintained and reviewed regularly for updates as necessary. Our goal is to utilize this system to promote consistency through the company for our web and print content. Changes should be communicated in a manner than reaches all areas within responsibility.

Primary Logo Colors

Dark Blue, HEX #262262

For the logo's primary color, a dark blue was selected that has a hint of purple. This color represents trust, smart, natural. This hint of purple we selected to mean creative as well.

Light Green, HEX #8DC63F

For the logo's complementing primary color, a lighter green was selected that has a hint of yellow. This color represents environment, life, grown, and innovation

Secondary Logo Colors

White, HEX #FFFFFF

Standard color for black and white, and inverted views

Black, HEX #00000

For black and white views and inverted that are used when logos are printed without color in some cases

Light Gray, HEX #A7A9AC

For black and white views that are used when logos are printed without color in some cases
